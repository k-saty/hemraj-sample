About Us

Hemraj Group stands as the Largest Processor of Rice and Related Products in Eastern India, with a commanding presence in both international and domestic markets. Our main products include Parboiled Rice, Physically Refined Rice-Bran Oil, Crude Rice-Bran Oil, and De-oiled Rice-Bran Extraction.

We are proud to be an ISO 9001:2015, ISO 22000:2005, ISO 45001:2018, HACCP, GMP, FSSAI, APEDA, FIEO certified organization and a recognized Three Star Export House.
